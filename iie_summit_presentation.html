iFiNN 6. Innovation Description
Novelty

Most existing retail tools use simple keyword matching (e.g., "profit" = good). iFiNN uses Contextual AI.

Semantic Disambiguation
Distinguishing "Apple" the company from "apple" the fruit, and understanding "Profit fell less than expected" as a potentially positive signal.
Multimodal Analysis
Correlation of Option Chain data (OI shifts) with News Sentiment in real-time.

iFiNN 8. Research Background
Scientific Foundation

Our approach relies on state-of-the-art Natural Language Processing (NLP) models, specifically BERT and FinBERT, fine-tuned for Indian financial contexts.
