iFiNN Model Parameter Marketplace

Your Models, Your Revenue — Powered by Our Ecosystem

A Hugging Face-style marketplace for trained model parameters with auction mechanics and transparent licensing.

How It Works
1. Train Your Model
Use our platform to configure hyperparameters, train on your strategy, validate performance
2. List on Marketplace
Set your price or enable auction mode for bidding. Specify usage rights (personal, commercial, API)
3. Buyers Browse & Purchase
Micro-transactions via payment gateway. Instant deployment to buyer's account
4. Revenue Sharing
Creator earns 70-85%, iFiNN takes 15-30% commission. Transparent, automated payouts

Auction Mechanics

Time-Limited Auctions: Bid on exclusive access to top-performing models
Buyout Options: Instant purchase at fixed price or wait for auction end
