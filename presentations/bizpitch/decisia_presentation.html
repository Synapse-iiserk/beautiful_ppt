The Core Problem in Retail Trading

Retail users face institutional-grade complexity without institutional-grade tools.

Current Pain Points
  • X
    Black-box platforms
    Predictions are delivered, but users cannot audit logic or assumptions.
  • X
    No model configurability
    Hyperparameters are locked for non-technical traders.
  • X
    Fragmented workflows
    Charts, sentiment, strategy testing, and execution live in separate tools.
Market Consequences
  • X
    Institutional pricing barriers
    Premium research stacks cost over INR 20 Lakhs/year.
  • X
    Signal-to-noise breakdown
    Retail decisions rely on noise, not validated and contextual signals.
  • X
    Low decision confidence
    Most users cannot explain why a trade was taken.

The Gap We Address

iFiNN bridges this divide with transparent AI, no-code model tuning, and integrated execution intelligence.
