2026 Strategy

Sharpe Ratio (Core Ranking Metric)
S = (Rₐ − Rₑ) / σRₐ
Rₐ = asset return  |  Rₑ = risk-free rate  |  σ = standard deviation of excess return
Maximum Drawdown (Risk Guard)
MDD = (Pₜtrough − Pₜpeak) / Pₜpeak
Automatic circuit breaker triggers when MDD exceeds user-set threshold during backtesting & live trading.
Sortino Ratio (Downside Risk)
Sₑ = (Rₐ − T) / σd
T = target return  |  σd = downside deviation. Penalises only negative volatility unlike Sharpe.

Real-Time Social Sentiment Intelligence

Innovation #2

Social discourse precedes mainstream financial news by 2–6 hours. iFINN's "Social First" engine ingests posts across Reddit, Twitter/X, Discord, and Telegram, runs them through a domain-tuned FinBERT NLP pipeline, clusters emerging narratives by topic, and outputs a price-correlated sentiment score — enabling traders to front-run FOMO cycles and meme stock runs before they hit Bloomberg.

📊 Real Example: GME-style Event in India

iFINN detected r/IndiaInvestments & Telegram buzz on a mid-cap pharma stock 3.5 hours before it broke on CNBC. Sentiment score jumped from 0.21 → 0.78. Users with alerts bought in 8% below the day's high, closing position at +14.3%.

B2B Client Playbook — Hedge Funds, HFT & Enterprise

Phase 1–2

iFINN approaches institutional clients as a boutique specialist, not a feature-bloated suite. By targeting 2–3 anchor clients in each vertical, iFINN builds audited track records before mass-market launch. The financial model targets institutional API and marketplace revenue reaching ₹3L (FY27) → ₹15L (FY28) from enterprise contracts alone.

⚡ High-Frequency Trading Firms
  • Pain: Latency arbitrage in Indian markets is under-served. Co-location at NSE's Powai DC is expensive; strategy alpha decays fast.
  • iFINN Offer: Sub-1ms WebSocket signal stream, CUDA GPU model inference, option chain analytics & direct market data feeds.
  • Pricing: ₹5–15L/year Enterprise API contract. Volume-based after 10M calls/month.
  • Target FY26 Anchor: 1 Indian HFT desk (bootstrapped via IISER network & SEBI sandbox)
💰 Hedge Funds & AIF Managers
  • Pain: SEBI AIF regulations require explainable model predictions for compliance. Bloomberg lack of domestic sentiment intelligence.
  • iFINN Offer: Full ML transparency pipeline, Sharpe/Sortino live benchmarking, sector-specific (Healthcare, ESG, DeFi) alpha signals.
  • Pricing: ₹3–8L/year SaaS license + ₹0.50/API call for data.
  • Target FY27: 2–3 Cat-II/III AIF managers via GIFT City IFSC hub.
🏢 Corporates & Banks (White-Label)
  • Pain: Mid-size banks & NBFCs want AI analytics but lack ML infra or data scientists. Building in-house costs ₹5Cr+ annually.
  • iFINN Offer: White-label iFINN engine embedded in their platform. Custom-branded UI, bank's own brand, iFINN's ML backend.
  • Pricing: ₹10–50L/year license + ₹2–5L onboarding. Revenue share option.
  • Target FY27–28: 1 mid-tier bank or NBFC. Introductions via TiE & NEN networks.
